Welcome to Jesse's Marvel CRUD RESTful Web API

You can store your favorite Marvel characters and information about them, including "name", "description", "comics_appeared_in" (an integer), and "super_power".

  • After you sign in, click 'Profile' then 'Details' to get your personal token.

  • Use header: "x-access-token" and value: "Bearer your-token" where your-token is your personal token.
  • Use JSON to create or update characters.
